**根因分析**:

**调用链**:
1. `ci/build.sh:165` → `python3.10 setup.py build bdist_wheel`

3. `torch_npu/CMakeLists.txt:85` 设置 `set(CMAKE_CXX_STANDARD 17)`
4. ninja 调用 `c++ ... -std=c++17 ...` 编译 `torch_npu/csrc/aten/CustomRegisterSchema.cpp`
5. 该源文件 `#include` 安装的 PyTorch(`/opt/conda/envs/py_3.10/lib/python3.10/site-packages/torch/include/ATen/ATen.h`)
